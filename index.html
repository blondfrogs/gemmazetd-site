Why this game exists

Gem TD is one of the most beloved custom maps ever made — born in Warcraft III, kept alive for nearly two decades in the Dota 2 Arcade. But it never had a real home on mobile.

This is that home: the place-five-keep-one heartbeat, the authentic crafting recipes, the Dota-style aura stacking and deferred damage — rebuilt piece by piece and tuned by hand, not ported. If you've ever kept a terrible gem just because the wall was in the perfect spot, this was made for you.
